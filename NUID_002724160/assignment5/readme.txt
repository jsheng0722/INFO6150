purpose of the website:
	We know that there are plenty of sites out there to help you find a job, as well as sites where users post tasks 
for seeking the help of more professional people. My idea is that there will always be times in life when you need other 
people's help. There will always be things that are difficult for you but easy for others. If you post a task, put it on the 
website and let other people know about it, it might have someone to help you solve the problem you're facing.
