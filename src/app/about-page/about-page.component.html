EastT has launched as a convenient, fast, and non-profit task posting platform.

F.A.Q

EasyT is a platform that allows users to post tasks or projects they need help with and find freelancers or service providers to complete the work.

A wide range of tasks can be posted on EasyT, including graphic design, writing and translation, video and audio production, data entry, and many others.

Users can create an account on the website and post their task by providing details about the work, the budget, and the deadline. Freelancers or service providers can then submit proposals to complete the work. The task poster can choose the most suitable candidate based on their portfolio, ratings, and reviews.

The benefits of using EasyT include the ability to find high-quality, affordable freelance services, access to a large pool of skilled professionals, and the ability to easily manage projects and payments through the platform.

Most EasyT have measures in place to ensure the safety of users' information and payments. This may include secure payment systems, escrow services, and dispute resolution processes.

EasyT typically has built-in payment systems, such as PayPal or credit card processing, that allow users to securely pay for tasks. The payment is usually held in escrow until the work is completed to the satisfaction of the task poster.
